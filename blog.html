UX UW Blog Contribution

The UX@UW Blog is where our community shares thoughts about User Experience design, covering everything from tools to process.

Do you have UX thoughts you want to share with other UX-minded individuals? Send a brief proposal of your article to ux@uw.edu. Make it clear in the subject line that are you interested in contributing to the blog.

Contribution Requirements

Here are some simple guidelines:

  • The connection to UX is straightforward.
  • The topic is of concern to the general UX@UW community.
  • The focus of the article is on building good UX and not on marketing or commercializing products.
  • You are not trying to sell a product or service to the readers.
  • Writing needs to be clear, concise and generally free of grammatical errors
  • Correct citations for outside sources if there are any
