UX UW Blog Contribution

The UX@UW Blog is where UX professionals from the University of Washington share their thoughts about UX design with other UX-affiliated UW individuals. Do you have interesting UX thoughts you want to share with other UX-minded individuals? Write them down and share them with us!

Contribution Requirements

So what does it take for your contribution to be selected? Here are some simple guidelines:

  • The connection to UX has to be clear
  • Writing needs to be clear, concise and free of grammatical errors
  • Correct citations for outside sources if there are any

Submitting Contributions

Please submit your contributions as a PDF, online post, or viewable Google Doc to ux@uw.edu. Make it clear in the subject line that are you interested in contributing.
