UX UW Blog Contribution

The UX@UW Blog is where UX designers from the University of Washington share their thoughts about UX design. Do you have UX thoughts you want to share with other UX-minded individuals? Write them down and share them with us!

Contribution Requirements

What are some things we look for in contributions? Here are some simple guidelines:

  • The connection to UX is straightforward
  • Writing needs to be clear, concise and free of grammatical errors
  • Correct citations for outside sources if there are any
  • any more??

Submitting Contributions

Please submit your contributions as a PDF, online post, or viewable Google Doc (not sure what the format should be) to ux@uw.edu. Make it clear in the subject line that are you interested in contributing to the blog.
