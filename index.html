UX @ UW

This site is to provide UX resources from experienced UX designers for the University of Washington UX community.

Blog

Curious to learn more about UX design from experienced University of Washington UX designers? Feel free to check out our Medium Blog. If you are interested in contributing to the blog, please check out our contribution page.

Help Sessions

Interested in getting UX help from UX professionals regarding you UW associated project? Look no further! Our UX community provides free consultations every Thursday from 3:00-4:00. You can find more details and the signup form here.
