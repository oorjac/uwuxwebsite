UX @ UW

This site was created by University of Washington (UW) UX designers that wanted to provide UX resources for the UW UX community, as well as those who want an introduction to UX. These resources consist of UX consultations, and the UX@UW blog.

Consulations

UX designers here at UW provide free UX consultations every Thursday from 3:00-4:00. To learn more about these consultations, please check here.


Blog

Curious to learn more about UX design from UW UX designers? Feel free to check out the Medium Blog. If you are interested in contributing to the blog, please check out our contribution page.
