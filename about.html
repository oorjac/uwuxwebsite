UX UW Community

The UX@UW site is an effort by members of the UW UX community, promoting the importance of User Experience (UX) Design in the creation of tools and applications that benefit the University of Washington’s students, faculty and researchers in the pursuit of learning, teaching and research. UX@UW’s contributors are design professionals from different units in UW, including Academic Experience Design & Delivery (AXDD), Application Infrastructure Engineering, Office of Research Information (ORIS) and the Kuali Student Project.
